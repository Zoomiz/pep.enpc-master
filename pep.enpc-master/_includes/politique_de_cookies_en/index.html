What are cookies ?

A cookie is a small text file that is stored by your browser on your device, such as your computer or mobile phone, when you visit a website and can send information back to that site. This information is most often used to facilitate your navigation, for example by remembering your choice of language.
However, cookies may be used for a variety of purposes, such as collecting statistics about the way you use a site, enhancing site security or displaying information correctly.

Do we use cookies?

We use cookies on several pages of our Site and this section indicates the different types of cookies we use and for what purposes.

Operating cookies
Operational cookies are those that we add to the Website ourselves. We use these cookies to ensure that our site works properly from a technical point of view, for instance when using web forms, and to collect statistics for website traffic analysis.
For these purposes, the Website of Ponts Etudes Projets uses Google Analytics, a web analysis service provided by Google, Inc. These cookies only produce anonymous statistics and traffic volumes, excluding any individual information, and allow us to better understand our Website and improve our services, by establishing statistics and traffic volumes (sections and content visited, path, etc.). The terms of use of Google Analytics specify in particular that Google does not establish a link between the IP address of the person concerned and other data held by Google. The lifetime of these audience measurement cookies does not exceed 13 months.
For further information on how Google handles this data, please visit : https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/6004245?hl=en

Other cookies
We maintain several social media platforms and offer users the ability to join us on these platforms or post content by clicking on the social media icons on our Website. Moreover, some of the Website's pages deliver our content through the embedding of videos from these social media platforms.
To provide these services, cookies from social media platforms, such as Twitter, Google and YouTube, are used on some of our web pages. If you have any questions about the use of third-party cookies, we recommend that you read the cookie policies published on the websites of these platforms.

How to manage cookies?

By using our Website, you consent to the use of audience measurement cookies and social network cookies. However, you can choose at any time to disable all or some of the audience measurement cookies or social network cookies. You can delete all cookies already stored on your computer. Your browser can also be set to alert you to cookies stored on your computer and ask you to accept them or not.
If you decide to delete your cookies, please be aware that any preferences you may have set will be lost. Furthermore, blocking all cookies will mean that many websites (including ours) will no longer function properly.

Declining an audience cookie
Our website uses cookies from Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. If you do not want our website to store cookies in your browser for web analytics purposes, you can refuse the use of Google Analytics cookies by downloading and installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
